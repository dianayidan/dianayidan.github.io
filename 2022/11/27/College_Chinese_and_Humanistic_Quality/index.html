Cultivation of College Chinese and Humanistic Quality

Introduction:

As a contemporary college student, in addition to learning professional knowledge, we should also pay more attention to the cultivation of cultural quality, including emotional intelligence, the way of dealing with people, and understanding of the world. Understanding these indirect factors can enable contemporary college students to better integrate into society and make reasonable choices for their lives. Professional courses are of importance to college students, learning professional knowledge and technology can make them more competitive in future work, make greater contributions to society, and become the backbone of the country. But what cannot be ignored is the cultivation of the humanistic quality of college students. The cultivation of humanistic quality can guide students on how to behave, including how to deal with the relationship between man and nature, man and society, and between different people including their rationality, emotion, will, and other issues. Humanist scholars and artists advocate humanity against divinity, human rights against theocracy, and individual freedom against personal attachment.
